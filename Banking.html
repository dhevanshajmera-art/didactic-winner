Personal Accout

Deposit and withdrawal:
You can deposit cash, checks, or electronic transfers and withdraw funds through ATMs,online,or at a branch.

Interest:
Savings accounts allow you to save money while earning interest on your balance.

Debit cards:
A debit card is often provided for purchases, cash withdrawals, and online transactions.

Online and mobile banking:
Many banks offer online and mobile platforms for 24/7 account management, including checking balances, transferring money, and making payments.
Payment services:
You can use your account for various payments, such as rent, utility bills, and credit card bills, often through services like NEFT, RTGS, or IMPS.
